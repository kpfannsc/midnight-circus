The Midnight Circus

Primoris Renewable Energy • 2025 Holiday Party

The Arch, Denver • December XX, 2025 • 6:00 PM – 12:00 AM

An elegant night of wonder, spectacle, and celebration.

About the Night

Step into The Midnight Circus—an immersive celebration designed to mesmerize. This year, we’re transforming The Arch into a cinematic experience that blends the elegance of a black-tie gala with the intrigue of a reimagined circus.

From rich performances to dramatic transitions and ambient lounge spaces, every detail is curated to surprise and delight. Expect moody lighting, velvet textures, crafted cocktails, gourmet food, and unexpected encounters.

No clichés. No clowns. Just mystery, glamour, and unforgettable moments.

Schedule & Experiences

6:00 PM – Come One, Come All

Cocktail hour with a dramatic sidewalk entrance, aerial champagne server, tattoo or henna artist, and interactive décor like photo ops, cutouts, and a strongman stand.

7:00 PM – Step Right Up

Guests take their seats for a heartfelt welcome, committee introductions, a special award presentation, and speeches. Visuals include a central stage flanked by screens.

7:30 PM – The Main Event

A seated multi-course dinner paired with a fiery main performance act. After the show, the mood shifts to ambient music.

9:00 PM to Midnight – Backstage Pass

The after-party begins with raffle prizes, live entertainers (juggler, caricature artist, fortune teller), stilt performers guarding a dramatic tunnel entrance, light-up dance floor, whiskey tasting, cigar rolling, hookah bar, dessert lounge, and a glowing back bar.

Dress Code Inspiration

The Midnight Circus is more than a party—it’s an invitation to enter a cinematic world of mystery, drama, and elegance. We encourage bold, beautiful attire that transforms each guest into part of the experience.

Think rich jewel tones, velvet, satin, and metallic accents. Floor-length gowns, dark florals, embroidered jackets, moody glam, and layered textures. This is your chance to be dramatic, elegant, and expressive.

Please avoid cliché circus elements like stripes or clownwear. Instead, lean into elevated, theatrical fashion—refined and mysterious.
