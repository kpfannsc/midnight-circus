Welcome to the Midnight Circus

Tonight, The Arch transforms into a world of rich color, golden light, and unforgettable spectacle. From the moment you arrive, you will be part of an experience designed to surprise, delight, and draw you deeper into its spell.

Expect artistry that defies the ordinary. Feats of balance and grace, light that moves like liquid through the dark, and performers who appear where you least expect them. Every turn offers a new scene, every moment a chance to be caught in the wonder of it all.

This is a celebration of the people who power Primoris Renewable Energy. Your skill and dedication have built more than projects, you have built moments worth honoring. Tonight we honor that spirit with the presentation of the Steve Larsen Award, an evening of performance that leaves the ordinary far behind, and a party where the music, laughter, and energy carry us well past the final bow.

Style for the Spectacle

The Midnight Circus is not just an event, it is a setting where every guest adds to the atmosphere. Think of it as a chance to step into a role for the evening and choose something that makes you feel part of the story.

Deep, luxurious colors, textured fabrics, and a touch of shimmer or metallic detail can feel right at home here. Instead of circus stripes, imagine a jacket with intricate detail, a gown in rich jewel tones, or a suit with an unexpected flourish. Accessories can be subtle or bold, and a well-chosen masquerade mask can be the perfect finishing touch.

We will transform the venue into a Midnight Circus itself: a world of shadow and light, velvet and gold. Arrive in attire that makes you feel at home in that world, ready to step into the scene and become part of the night’s unfolding spectacle.

Travel & Access

The Midnight Circus will unfold at The Arch – 3001 Walnut St, Denver, CO, a space transformed for one night only into a world of shadow, light, and celebration. You can find the venue on the map below.

Parking
